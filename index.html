Background...

Hi! I'm very glad to see you here! I'm Octavio Villalpando, a Public Accountant graduated from FACPYA, UANL with almost 20 years of experience in finance, accounting, tax regulation, management processes, etc.

Since 10 years ago, I switched from Accounting to IT, working as a Business Analyst, Product Owner and IT Advisor for John Deere México.

Currently I'm pursuing new horizons by completing the Software Engineering Immersive Program from General Assembly, so I can add developer skills to my current portfolio and become a better resource as a link in between final users and its business language and IT Developers and its own technical language.

About...

I have lived in Monterrey city, at the north of México almost all my life. When I'm not doing things from work, I enjoy a lot to spend time with my fiancée and my seven cats and my dog, plus a stray cat colony that I feed and care for.

Also, sometimes I use my comunication skills giving lectures about literature and genre cinema in different events around the country, which is something y really enjoy!
